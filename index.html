💡 Tips to Improve Your Typing Speed

👆 Use all ten fingers whenever possible — proper finger placement makes a big difference.
👀 Keep your attention on the text you're typing instead of looking down at the keys.
🎯 Prioritize accuracy first — speed naturally improves with repetition.
⏸️ Ease your pace slightly when approaching punctuation marks and capital letters.
🔄 Revisit phrases that consistently slow you down to build confidence and consistency.
🧘 Keep your hands and wrists relaxed — unnecessary tension can reduce speed and comfort.
